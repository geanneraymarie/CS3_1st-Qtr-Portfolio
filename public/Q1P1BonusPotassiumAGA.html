cheesy potato balls 🤤 ⋆˚✿˖°

i absolutely love this recipe since it's very straightforward and easy to do, but of course, my main reason is because it tastes absolutely DELICIOUS 💖😍

what you'll need :

  • 3 boiled potatoes
  • a teaspoon of garlic powder
  • a few pinches of salt
  • a bowl of bread crumbs for frying
  • some cheese for the filling
  • some oil for deep frying

    • note : these would make 2-3 servings ✧˖°

⤷ ゛instructions ˎˊ˗

  1. boil the potatoes until they are soft
  2. in a bowl, mash the boiled potatoes
  3. add garlic powder and salt to the mashed potatoes and mix well
  4. prepare a bowl of bread crumbs for coating the potato balls
  5. cut the cheese into small pieces for the filling
  6. heat oil in a deep frying pan
  7. take scoops of mashed potato and flatten in your hand
  8. place a piece of cheese in the center and wrap the potato around it to form a ball
  9. roll the potato ball in the bread crumbs until fully coated
  10. carefully place the coated potato balls into the hot oil and fry until golden brown
  11. remove the fried potato balls and place them on paper towels to drain excess oil
  12. serve hot and enjoy!
